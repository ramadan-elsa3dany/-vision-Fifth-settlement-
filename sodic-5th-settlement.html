Villas


The villas at The Estates exemplify architectural grandeur, offering expansive living spaces and lavish finishes. Townhouses


For those seeking a more compact yet refined living space, the townhouses at The Estates are an ideal choice. Luxury and Affordability Combined


A notable aspect of The Estates is its pricing strategy, which ensures that luxury living is attainable for a wide range of homebuyers. With prices starting from 21,333 Egyptian Pounds per square meter and ranging up to 25,388 Egyptian Pounds per square meter, The Estates offers exceptional value for money compared to similar high-end residential compounds in the region.


Flexible Payment System


Recognizing the importance of financial flexibility in realizing the dream of homeownership, The Estates offers a convenient payment system. Prospective buyers can secure their desired property with a downpayment of only 10% of the total unit price. The remaining balance can be paid off over a comfortable period of 6 to 8 years in installments. This flexible payment plan allows homeowners to budget effectively and transform their aspirations of owning a luxurious home into a reality.

About the developer 

SODIC, a prominent real estate developer in Egypt, has established an impressive portfolio of mixed-use communities in West Cairo, East Cairo, and the North Coast. Since its inception in 1996, SODIC has consistently delivered exceptional developments, garnering recognition and leading the way in creating expansive mixed-use communities in the emerging suburban areas surrounding Cairo.

SODIC's meticulously planned and spacious developments on both the western and eastern sides of Cairo remain at the forefront of the growing demand for relocations away from the heavily congested central Cairo. The company's projects are renowned for their exceptional quality, innovative design, and unwavering commitment to sustainable development.
