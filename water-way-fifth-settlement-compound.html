The Waterway Compound is a highly esteemed residential complex that offers its residents an extravagant and prestigious lifestyle, thanks to its modern architectural design and well-appointed amenities.
Living in the Waterway Compound in New Cairo has become a popular choice among buyers seeking a wide range of residential units available at unbeatable prices and flexible payment plans.
If you're ready to secure a promising future for your family, explore the currently available units for sale in Waterway New Cairo.

water way location 

The Waterway Compound is highly regarded for its prime location, situated near northern N Teseen Street in the Fifth Settlement, with close proximity to Cairo Festival City. This advantageous location establishes a strong connection to the New Capital City, making it an attractive choice for both property buyers and investors.

The advantage of water way compound

 One of the key advantages of the Waterway Compound in New Cairo is its exceptional accessibility to various essential facilities in the surrounding neighborhood, including educational, governmental, financial, and commercial establishments

the places near by water way compound 

Noteworthy places near the Waterway Compound include the American University, which is only a 15-minute drive away. Additionally, Nasr City is approximately 20 minutes away from the compound. The location also offers convenient access to both Cairo-Suez Road and Cairo-Sokhna Road. For shopping needs, residents can easily reach the accessible commercial centers of Mostakbal Mall and Wadi Degla Mall.

The Waterway Compound boasts a substantial land area of 26 Feddan, contributing to its distinction. Residents of the compound enjoy a luxurious living experience with abundant green spaces integrated into a contemporary setting.

Architecturally, the Waterway in New Cairo showcases remarkable designs that harmonize with the modern lifestyle prevalent in the area.


Lastly, the compound features a magnificent artificial lake and charming small fountains, providing residents with picturesque views that enhance the overall appeal of the community.

The project owner water way compound 

The Waterway Company for development holds the official ownership of the Water Way compound, having established a partnership with a prominent real estate firm called "Equity Company." Equity Company is a subsidiary group of el Swedy Company, known for its achievements in the market. This successful collaboration between Waterway developments and Equity Group has positioned the compound as one of the most prestigious communities in New Cairo. It provides a diverse range of residential options at competitive prices, further enhancing its appeal in the real estate market.

the prices and payment plans 

The attractive prices of the Waterway New Cairo have motivated many prospective buyers to take a significant step towards securing their future housing experience. For those seeking a luxurious living environment, the opportunity to purchase a unit in the Waterway Compound is highly enticing.

To provide convenience to its customers, the Waterway development offers two different installment plans:

The first plan involves a down payment of 30%, along with a 2-year installment period.
The second plan requires a down payment of 40% in the second payment, with the remaining cost spread over 4 years.
By offering these flexible payment options, the Waterway Compound aims to facilitate the purchasing process and accommodate the diverse financial needs of its customers.
