Types of  Units in Hyde Park New Cairo

Hyde Park New Cairo offers a diverse range of housing choices, catering to the specific preferences of customers. These options include penthouses, villas, townhouses, twin houses, and terrace gardens. The master plan of Hyde Park is highly regarded due to its extensive number of residential units, surpassing 20,000 in total.

Of significant importance is the wide range of spaces available for each type of unit, allowing buyers to select the one that best suits their individual requirements.

The developer of Hyde Park New Cairo

Hyde Park Company, established in 2007 with Emirates Damak and the Egyptian Housing and Development Bank as its main shareholders, has successfully undertaken several prominent projects in Egypt, notably including Hyde Park New Cairo.

All Hyde Park Development communities share a common vision of creating a harmonious blend of social life, work, and entertainment within a neighborhood that seamlessly integrates urban vibrancy with natural simplicity.

It is worth mentioning that Hyde Park's investments reached 5 billion at the beginning of 2017, driven by its remarkable achievement of selling all its units within a short period of time.

As of the end of 2020, the company has sold over 3,800 residential units in the Hyde Park community in the fifth settlement, generating sales worth 2.5 billion EGP.
